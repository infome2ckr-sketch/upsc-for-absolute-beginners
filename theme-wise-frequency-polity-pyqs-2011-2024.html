Start with the 4 red themes first. They alone cover 88 questions — over 60% of all Polity PYQs. Master Parliament and FRs from Laxmikanth before touching blue themes.

Key Observations

Parliament is king

~30 questions in 14 years. Expect 2-4 every year. Master structure, procedures, bills first.

FRs second pillar

22 questions. Right to Privacy (Art 21) repeated in 2018, 2021, 2024. Clear favourite.

Framework every year

GoI Acts 1919, 1935 and Article 368 amendment procedure are perennial.

Executive + Judiciary = 30+ Qs

Presidential election vote values and SC jurisdiction are frequently tested.

Shift since 2023

Statement I/II format demands analytical understanding, not rote.

Applied concepts rising

Due Process, Rule of Law, Constitutional Government up since 2019.

Local Governance comeback

73rd/74th, PESA, Sixth Schedule tested in 2023-2024. Renewed interest.

Start today. Stay consistent.

Focus 80% on red themes first. Do the 4-hour routine for 30 days. Do not buy more books. Build the habit, then expand.
