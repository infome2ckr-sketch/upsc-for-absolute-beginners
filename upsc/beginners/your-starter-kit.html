UPSC Beginner Guide • Part 2

Your Starter Kit

Coaching sells 50 books. You need 6.

Total cost ~₹2,500 (less second-hand). Don't buy optional books yet. Depth > breadth.

01

Print the Syllabus

This is your GPS. Every question comes from here. Stick it above your desk. Read 2 mins daily.

02

Last 10 Years PYQs

Download Prelims + Mains papers. Don't solve yet. Read 1 Prelims paper today. Notice the shift to 'how many pairs' format.

03

M. Laxmikanth Polity (7th)

Covers ~15% Prelims + most of GS2 Mains. Start Chapter 1: Historical Background.

04

History NCERT + Spectrum

NCERT Class 12 Themes Part 1-3 + Spectrum Modern India. Start 10 pages/day from Themes Part 1.

05

Geography NCERT + GC Leong

Class 11 Fundamentals of Physical Geography + GC Leong. Begin with Class 11 Chapter 2.

06

Current Affairs

The Hindu OR Indian Express + 1 monthly compilation (Vision/Insights). Today: front page + 1 editorial only.

Foundation Month

Your first 30-day plan

Goal is not to finish syllabus. Goal is to kill fear and build habit.

4-Hour Daily Routine

Hour 1

Newspaper (60m)

syllabus-linked news, 1 editorial, 1 international. Max 5 bullets.

Hour 2-3

Static (120m)

Week 1-2 Polity Laxmikanth Ch 1-10 (8 pages/day, read twice). Week 3-4 History NCERT (10 pages/day).

Hour 4a

Revision (30m)

revise yesterday.

Hour 4b

PYQs (30m)

2 questions from today's chapter. Expect to get wrong.

Active Recall

After every chapter, close book, write 5 points you remember.

Weekends

1 sectional Prelims test (Polity/History) from any free site.

Zero Days Policy

Even if sick, read 1 page. Keep the chain unbroken.
