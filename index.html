01

I'm not from English medium / IIT / DU

UPSC doesn't care where you're from. The results saw everyone from the children of daily wagers to IPS officers topping the list. Whether it's Pawan Kumar, who cleared in Hindi medium, or toppers from small-town backgrounds, the exam checks your depth of understanding, not your accent or your college's brand name.

02

I need to study 15 hours a day

You don't. You need four to five quality hours with full focus. There are plenty of toppers who cleared this exam with a full-time job using this exact routine. Consistency beats 'one-day-intensity' every single time.

03

I need coaching or I'll fail

Coaching only gives you structure, not selection. In 2026, you can build that same structure with YouTube, standard books, and discipline for almost nothing. That's what this guide will do for you.
