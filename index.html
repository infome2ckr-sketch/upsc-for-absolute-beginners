THE 3 FEARS

📚

Too Many Books

Everyone lists 50 books. You need NCERTs (6-12) first, then ONE standard book per subject. That's it for 6 months.

🧠

Forgetting

You will forget. Everyone does. UPSC is not memory, it's revision. Plan for 3 readings, not 1 perfect one.

⏳

Wasting Time

Without a start date and daily target, you'll research forever. Give yourself a 90-day foundation block.

You don't need more content.
You need a starting line.

Pick NCERTs. Make a timetable. Read 2 hours daily. Revise on Sunday. Repeat for 90 days. That's Day 1 to Day 90.

Foundation First. Everything Else Later.
